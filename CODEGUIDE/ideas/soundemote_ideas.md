# Soundemote Ideas

Short prompts only. Expand later when an idea becomes a task.

## Website Gravity

- Prettyscope Online: browser scope playground with sound, color, glow, decay.
- Signal Lab: learn synthesis by hearing and seeing waves.
- Seed Lab: one-click song seed generator for producers.
- Beat Lab: random electronic percussion patterns with WAV/MIDI downloads.
- Visualizer commissions page: custom signal-reactive clips for artists.
- Hydrus waitlist: careful flagship synth reveal, no rushed hype.

## Generators

- Generate drums, bass, chords, lead, tempo, key, tags.
- Regenerate one part without changing the whole seed.
- Export MIDI first; WAV/stems later.
- Deterministic seed links for sharing.
- Local favorites before accounts.
- Mutation and crossbreeding later.

## Brand Lines

- The tools are gravity. The art is the offer.
- Learn the sounds inside electronic music.
- Electronic music has instruments too.
- See sound as signal art.
- Press one button. Get a musical starting point.
- The ecosystem proves the artistry.

## Guardrails

- Do not fence off the ecosystem too early.
- Do not make free tools feel like crippleware.
- Do not rush Hydrus.
- Do not beg for attention.
- Show proof: sound, visuals, code, finished artifacts.
- Keep copy short and useful.
